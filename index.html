📐 How We Calculate Environmental Impact

🔋 Energy Totals

Daily Total

Calculated by summing all 5-minute power readings from today's CSV data, converting watts to kilowatt-hours.

Energy (kWh) = Σ (Power in Watts / 1000) × (5 minutes / 60)
Monthly Total

Accumulated from daily totals throughout the current month. Resets to zero at the start of each new month.

Lifetime Total

Started at 1,063,460 kWh (historical baseline) and grows by adding each day's total. Never resets.

🌍 Environmental Parameters

💨 CO₂ Avoided

Amount of carbon dioxide emissions prevented by using solar instead of grid electricity.

CO₂ Avoided = Energy (kWh) × 1 kg CO₂/kWh
🌳 Trees Equivalent

Number of trees needed to absorb the same CO₂. Based on 250-1000 trees per hectare absorbing 22 kg CO₂/year each.

Trees = CO₂ Avoided / (22 kg/tree/year ÷ Trees per hectare)
🏠 Households Powered

Years an average household could run on this energy, based on 3,546.63 kWh per household per year.

Household Years = Energy (kWh) / 3,546.63 kWh/year
🚗 Driving Equivalent

Distance you could drive a car that would emit the same CO₂, at 0.251 kg CO₂ per km.

Distance = CO₂ Avoided / 0.251033 kg/km
✈️ Flying Equivalent

Distance you could fly that would emit the same CO₂, at 12.28 kg CO₂ per km flown.

Distance = CO₂ Avoided / 12.280237 kg/km
⚫ Coal Saved

Amount of coal that would be needed to generate the same electricity, at 0.508 kg coal per kWh.

Coal Saved = Energy (kWh) × 0.50802304 kg/kWh
💧 Water Saved

Water saved by not using conventional power plants (which need cooling), at 1.4 litres per kWh.

Water Saved = Energy (kWh) × 1.4 litres/kWh

📊 Data Sources

Update Frequency

Data is fetched from the Genergy solar monitoring system every 5 minutes and automatically pushed to this dashboard.

Data Persistence

All totals are stored locally in persistent_totals.json to maintain accuracy across system restarts and ensure cumulative values are preserved.
